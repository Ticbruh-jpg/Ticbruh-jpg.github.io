Our Story

Built on Transparency,
Grown on Trust

LedgerStone & Associates was founded in 2012 by Margaret Holden and James Rourke, two professionals who believed that small and medium-sized businesses deserved the same financial clarity as large corporations — without the enterprise price tag.

Starting from a modest office on Fernwood Lane in Bristol, Margaret and James set out with a clear mission: bring radical transparency to bookkeeping. No jargon, no mystery, just clean numbers and honest conversations.

Over a decade later, we've served hundreds of clients across Bristol, Bath, and beyond — from independent tradespeople and creative studios to growing e-commerce brands and professional practices.

Our mission remains unchanged: to give every business owner the financial clarity they need to make confident decisions.

"We started LedgerStone because we saw too many good businesses struggling not because of what they made — but because they couldn't see clearly what they had."
— Margaret Holden & James Rourke, Co-Founders
The People Behind the Numbers

Meet the Team

Our small, dedicated team brings together decades of bookkeeping expertise and a genuine passion for financial clarity.

Margaret Holden

Founder & Principal Bookkeeper

With over 20 years of experience in financial record-keeping, Margaret oversees client relationships and ensures every set of books meets our exacting standards. She holds a Fellowship of the Institute of Certified Bookkeepers.

James Rourke

Co-Founder & Operations Director

James manages the day-to-day operations and client onboarding process. His background in corporate finance gives LedgerStone a strategic edge, helping clients understand their numbers in the context of their broader goals.

Priya Mehta

Junior Bookkeeper

Priya joined the team in 2021, bringing fresh energy and a meticulous eye for detail. Currently working towards her ICB certification, she handles day-to-day transaction recording and bank reconciliations for a portfolio of growing clients.
